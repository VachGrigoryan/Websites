Whooden Studios is an experimental creative studio, production company, and talent agency for brands, agencies, and institutions focused on culture, curation, and community. .

Bringing over 10 years of production, marketing, and strategy we partner with agencies, brands and institutions to curate, ideate, produce and activate groundbreaking stories told through digital, social and experiential activations while creating memorable cultural moments and building community. 

We choose to prioritize, genuine partnerships, strategic experimentation & creative integrity..
